About Us

Mining & Community Research is a nonprofit research group with core objective of finding solutions to issues in the extractive industry in Ghana and Africa to ensure sustainability in the sector. One key area is artisanal and small-scale mining.

To identify independent sources of funding to undertake research that benefits communities affected by extraction of natural resources.

To offer outstanding research that will assist communities and clients to make sustainable decisions.

To make use of innovative and current scientific methodologies to conduct research and produce compelling results.

OUR MISSION

We are here to:

  • Provide cutting edge research to aid mining communities and clients in making sustainable decisions.
  • Use innovative and modern scientific methodologies in conducting research and producing accurate results.
  • Identify independent sources of funding to undertake research that benefits communities affected by extraction of natural resources.
  • Develop and maintain a network of researchers involved in the global extractive community.
